Expectations

Campus

Thougth that campus would be like a this one. Big campus with great infrastructer. The area size would be like 100-300 acres

Classes

Thought that classroom will be elevated. Will be equipped with modern equipments.The chairs will be round and around the board.

Hostels

Thought that hostel rooms would be tiny. Without a lot of space.With only one window and shared washrooms.

Mess Food

Thought that mess food would be very bad and unhygienic.With very little nutrition.And almost no taste.

Reality

Campus

It was not that big.Not so very nice architecture.Decent space for roaming.But atleast have nice greens and concrete roads with footpaths.

Classes

The classes was not elevated.Very little space and was like school classroom.And have straight benches not round tables.

Hostel-room

The hostel rooms are like appartment with great facilities.They are spacious with flatmates not room mates.Gets cleaned between every 2-3days gap.

Mess food

The food is not the best but much better than what I have heard about the food of engineering college.With variety and mandatory items.
